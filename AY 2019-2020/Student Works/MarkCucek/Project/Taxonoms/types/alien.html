Miscellaneous Info

  • The Newcomer ability is exclusive to Alien types.
  • The Alien Crops field effect boosts Alien damage and doubles Alien damage dealt to taxonoms that resist Alien.
  • The Portal Matter Swirl field effect boosts Alien damage and boosts the defenses and speed of Alien types.
