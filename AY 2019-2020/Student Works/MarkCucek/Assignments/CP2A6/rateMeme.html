Video Memes

Bruh
Bruh Sound Effect #2, where the meme originated from.

The Bruh meme is one of utmost simplicity. It is only an audio recording of a man saying "bruh". When the meme was popular
people would frequently associate certain awkward or generally underwhelming moments as "bruh moments".
There was just something about saying it out loud that made it inherently silly.

Le Monke
UH OH, where the meme originated from.

The Uh Oh Stinky or Le Monke (whichever way you prefer to say it) meme features a very fat-looking monkey first farting and then saying "Uh Oh, Stinky!", and so on.
The original video was uploaded in early 2016, but only started to gain popularity in mid 2019

UH OH STINKY (Neapolitan version), one of the better Uh Oh Stinky parodies.

And Then The Mad Man Spontaneously Changes Into a Pickle

Here is a variation of the meme featuring three British men discussing how he turns himself into a pickle.

The meme is a reference to Pickle Rick from the show Rick and Morty. It is always told by a popular imaginary or real world character in various different situations.
