• Rules of the game:
  • 1. The king decides the question of the day.
  • 2. The king decides the time when everybody has to submit their answer.
  • 3. Everybody eagerly waits for the set time.
  • 4. When the time comes everybody writes their answers.
  • 5. The last person to write an answer loses.
  • 6. People with duplicate answer lose too.
  • 7. The entire room (or the king) then must decide what challenge losers should do.
  • Things to know:
  • 1. The king will set the time when creating the room. Remaining time will be displayed in the chatroom.
  • 2. The room will have it's unique link which can be shared for all the other players
  • 3. All the players and their answers are displayed in Player list window. It can be opened by pressing a button in chatroom's top-right corner
